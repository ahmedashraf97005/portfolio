Outcome & value

The dashboard highlights the main challenges, expectations, and demographics of professionals in data-related careers. By combining structured survey responses with interactive visualizations, the project uncovers insights such as the salary gap, career satisfaction levels, and role accessibility. This allows HR teams, managers, and aspiring data professionals to better understand trends, benchmark career progress, and design strategies for talent development in the data industry.
